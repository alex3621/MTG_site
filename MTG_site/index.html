Magic The Gathering

A Tabletop trading card game loved by millions world-wide

This website will be introducing you to my favorite game of all time, Magic the Gathering. Magic the Gathering, also known as MTG, is a sensational trading card game made by Wizards of the Coast.

Short introduction to the game

How to play?

Each player draws seven cards to start the game. With starting life total of 20 points. Players can play creature spells that are capable of reducing opponent's life points. The goal is to drop a player to 0 health points to win.
